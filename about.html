ABOUT US


MechAlchemy is where knowledge connects with inspiration. It is a multimedia, student-led project designed to provide insightful guidance in a world where the accessibility of reliable information only contributes to its neglect. We seek to provide every individual with the technical knowledge necessary to distinguish quality manufacturing from capitalist exploitation.


OUR TEAM


Our team consists of high school students across the US. Our talented individuals are responsible for carrying out necessary research, writing, and editing our content.
