MechAlchemy


MechAlchemy is where knowledge connects with inspiration. It is a multimedia, student-led project designed to provide insightful guidance in a world where the accessibility of information only contributes to its neglect. We seek to provide every individual with the technical knowledge necessary to distinguish quality manufacturing from capitalist exploitation.
